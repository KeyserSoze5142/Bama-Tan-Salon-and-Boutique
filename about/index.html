Hello, Gorgeous

More than a tan

Bama Tan has been part of the Tuscaloosa community for over two and a half decades. What started as a tanning salon has grown into a full glow-up destination — tanning and red light therapy, a hair and lash studio, and a boutique stocked with styles you'll actually want to wear.

We're women-owned, locally run, and we know most of our clients by name. Come in for a tan; leave with a new outfit and your new favorite salon.
