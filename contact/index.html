Good To Know

Frequently asked questions

Do I need an appointment to tan?

Walk-ins are welcome for tanning beds and red light therapy. Spray tans are by appointment only — call (205) 462-2115 to book.

What memberships do you offer?

Level 2 Club Tan is $55/month, Level 3 Club Tan is $65/month, and Club Spray is $65/month. Club Tan memberships are a 3-month commitment. One-month unlimited packages are also available per level.

Where are you located?

2337 University Blvd E, Tuscaloosa, AL 35404 — with parking right out front.
